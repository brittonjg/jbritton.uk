It's been a while since I updated this site. The old version served its purpose — a simple Flutter single-page app with a photo, some links, and a CV download. But I've been meaning to build something I can actually write on.

So here we are. A complete rebuild, from scratch, with Next.js 15 and a proper blog engine.

## Why rebuild?

The honest answer: I wanted a space to write about engineering leadership, the things I'm learning at Cuvva, and the occasional technical deep-dive. A static landing page wasn't cutting it.

## What's here

- **Blog** — you're reading it. MDX-powered, with syntax highlighting and custom components.
- **Experience** — a timeline of my career, pulled from structured data.
- **CV** — a web-readable resume with PDF download.

More to come. Thanks for visiting.
